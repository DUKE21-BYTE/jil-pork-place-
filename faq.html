Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Jil Pork Place.

Do you deliver?

Yes! We deliver to Ruaka, Runda, Gacharage, and nearby areas via boda. The delivery fee depends on the distance from our location in Ruaka. You can arrange your delivery instantly when ordering via WhatsApp.

How do I pay?

We accept M-Pesa. Our Buy Goods Till number is 753595. You can also pay via Cash when picking up your order from our restaurant.

Is the pork fresh?

100% Yes. We receive fresh stock daily for both our butchery and restaurant. Quality and hygiene are our absolute top priorities.
